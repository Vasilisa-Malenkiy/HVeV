# HVeV simulation

This is a HVeV-like detector simulation package developed for characterization of HVeV BeEST detectors. 

## Detector configuration

The HVeV detector consists of:

- **Silicon substrate:** 10 × 10 × 4 mm  
- **Top Al films (600 nm):** Split into inner and outer channels, each covering 50% of the top surface area  
  - Inner channel size: 7.07 × 7.07 mm  
- **Bottom Al film (variable thickness):** Covers the full bottom surface  
- **Source:** 7Be radioactive decay (electron capture) embedded in the Si substrate  

Phonons generated by the nuclear recoil propagate through the silicon crystal, reflecting off surfaces and being absorbed by the aluminum films via the KaplanQP quasiparticle cascade mechanism.
